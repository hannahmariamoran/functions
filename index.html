    ABOUT MOMENTO

    Introducing MOMENTO, a digital time capsule for capturing weekly moments in an image-based visual timeline. The project was born from the struggle of capturing meaningful moments in a fast-paced world. MOMENTO encourages users to pause and reflect by selecting one standout image per week, tagging it with date, location, and a brief description. It's about remembering the everyday, creating a living visual timeline to revisit life's journey.
